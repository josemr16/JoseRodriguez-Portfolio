  1. Food Chase
    2. Description:
    • Implemented a Full Stack JavaScript application that allows restaurants to make posts about foods that will get thrown away. They can sell the dish for a reduced price.
    • Users can sign up to view the food available, make a reservation and then proceed to pick it up at the restaurant location.
    • Utilized React.js and CSS for the frontend, Node + Express for the backend and PostgreSQL for the database.
    • Accessed liberties and packages including knex.js to connect database with backend, Bootstrap and Tachyons to style the frontend, and body-parser as middleware for the backend.
    Reasons:
    • According to USDA's Economic Research Service food waste is estimated at between 30 - 40 percent of the food supply.
    • 31% is approximately 133 billion pounds and 161 billion dollars worth of food.
    • Restaurant food waste amounts to 2 billion in lost profits.
    Interest:
    • Reduce food waste.
    • Decrease restaurant profit loss.
    • Connect people with amazing meals for an affordable price.
