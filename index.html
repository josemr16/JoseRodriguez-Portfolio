About Me


I am a computer science student at Lehman college currently going for my bachelor’s degree. I like many areas of computer science but the one I focus the most on is Front and Back end development.


I have a passion in technology which I discovered when I had my first computer around the age of 13. I always had questions about almost everything that had to do with technology. Questions like how is a webpage is created? How are computer programs created and how they know what to do? How does social media works? How there is an app for pretty much anything you want? That’s when I started having an interest in technology and what interest me the most is finding the answers that I always wanted to know and also the amazing things that can be done using technology.


I am a fun and creative guy. In my free time I like to spend time with my family, travel and discover new adventures.
